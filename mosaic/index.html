About Me

Mark Huasong Meng

I am a PhD student at School of Computing, National University of Singapore (NUS), under the joint supervision of Professor Dong Jin Song at NUS and Dr. Guo Huaqun at Institute for Infocomm Research (I2R), A*STAR. My research interests include cyber-security and software engineering.

Previously I severed as a Software Engineer at Gemalto (now part of Thales Group), a Software Analyst at NCS (a member of SingTel Group) and a Research Engineer at Institute for Infocomm Research, A*STAR. I received my Bachelor (Hons.) degree in Computer Science at Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore, and Master degree in Infocomm Security at National University of Singapore (NUS).

Prior to joining NUS for the PhD study, I have working experience in diverse product teams such as CAD software development, mobile banking solutions and e-government platform. My previous research covers security analysis on mobile applications and blockchain.
